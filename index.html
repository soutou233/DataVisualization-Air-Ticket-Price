Factors Affecting Air Ticket Prices

In this project, we aim to gain a deeper understanding of the factors that influence how flights are priced. In this area of price optimization, we analyze various types of alternative data that causes or co-relates with the coinciding rise/fall in intrinsic pricing of airfares.
